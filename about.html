About Us

What we are

SIT-Hub Center is a company based in Antalya, Turkey, whose mission is to enable students around the world to secure their future with quality education at a lower cost. Turkey has become in recent years a privileged destination of study for students from Africa, Asia and Latin America. The opening of the only country straddling Europe and Asia to the international education system has allowed Turkey to establish universities where education is based on the American system with English as the language of study. These universities provide education of equivalent quality to that of the United States and are distinguished from the latter by their very advantageous tuition fees. The diplomas and degrees obtained are also recognized all over the world. With these strengths, Turkey has been successful in attracting students from around the world and positioning itself as a worthy study destination worldwide.

Our primary goal is to place students in our partner universities where the language of education is English, by obtaining tuition discounts that are already beneficial for the quality of education provided. SIT-Hub Center also proposes English courses for students to develop their proficiency of the language before starting their academic studies. Our goal is to allow students who cannot afford universities with exorbitant tuition fees to have an opportunity to receive high quality education and thus be competitive in the global labor market. Students can also apply for the English courses without independently of the academic studies, and can prepare for exams such as TOEFL and IELTS. At SIT-Hub Center, success of the students is our first priority
